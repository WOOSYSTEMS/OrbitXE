Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2025

WOOSYSTEMS ("we", "our", or "us") operates OrbitXE. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Chrome extension and web-based remote control service.

1. Information We Collect

OrbitXE is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide the service:

  • Session Data: Temporary room IDs generated to connect your phone to your browser. These are randomly generated and not linked to any personal information.
  • Tab Information: Titles and URLs of your browser tabs are transmitted between your devices solely to enable the remote control functionality. This data is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve our service.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or content of web pages
  • Passwords or form data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Location data

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we process is used exclusively to:

  • Establish and maintain the connection between your phone and browser
  • Transmit control commands (mouse movements, keyboard input, etc.)
  • Display tab information on your phone remote

4. Data Transmission and Security

All data transmitted between your devices passes through our secure WebSocket servers using encrypted connections (WSS/HTTPS). Control commands and tab information are transmitted in real-time and are NOT stored or logged on our servers.

5. Data Retention

Session data is temporary and is automatically deleted when you disconnect or close your browser. We do not maintain any persistent records of your sessions or activities.

6. Third-Party Services

OrbitXE uses the following third-party services:

  • Railway: For hosting our server infrastructure
  • Chrome Web Store: For extension distribution

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their data practices.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete. You can stop using OrbitXE at any time by:

  • Closing the remote control page on your phone
  • Clicking "New Session" to end the current connection
  • Uninstalling the Chrome extension

8. Children's Privacy

OrbitXE is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
