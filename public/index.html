Everything You Need

A complete remote control solution for your browser

Wireless Trackpad

Use your phone screen as a trackpad. Swipe to move the cursor anywhere on screen, tap to click, and hold for right-click. It's like having a wireless mouse in your pocket.

Native Keyboard

Type using your phone's keyboard with autocorrect, swipe typing, and all your familiar features. No more squinting at tiny on-screen keyboards.

Smart Controls

OrbitXE auto-detects YouTube, Netflix, Zoom and other sites, showing relevant controls like play, pause, mute, skip, and fullscreen automatically.

Tab Management

Switch between browser tabs, open new tabs, and navigate to any URL - all from your phone. See all your open tabs and jump to any one instantly.

No App Required

Just scan a QR code with your phone camera and you're connected. Works on any phone with a web browser - iPhone, Android, anything.

Everything You Need

A complete remote control solution for your Mac

App-Aware Controls

OrbitXE Pro detects which app is active and shows relevant controls. PowerPoint gets slide controls, Spotify gets playback, Chrome gets browser controls - automatically.

Full Keyboard

Type using your phone's native keyboard with shortcuts like ⌘C, ⌘V, ⌘Z built in. Tab, Enter, Escape, and arrow keys all at your fingertips.

Media Controls

Control YouTube, Music, Spotify and more with play/pause, skip, seek, and volume controls that appear automatically when media apps are active.

Presenter Mode

Control PowerPoint and Keynote presentations wirelessly. Navigate slides, see your presenter notes on your phone, and never lose your place.

Instant Connect

Scan the QR code and you're connected. No app to download, no account to create. Works on any phone with a browser.
