Everything You Need

A complete remote control solution for your browser

Wireless Trackpad

Use your phone screen as a trackpad. Swipe to move the cursor anywhere on screen, tap to click, and hold for right-click. It's like having a wireless mouse in your pocket.

Native Keyboard

Type using your phone's keyboard with autocorrect, swipe typing, and all your familiar features. No more squinting at tiny on-screen keyboards.

Smart Controls

OrbitXE auto-detects YouTube, Netflix, Zoom and other sites, showing relevant controls like play, pause, mute, skip, and fullscreen automatically.

Tab Management

Switch between browser tabs, open new tabs, and navigate to any URL - all from your phone. See all your open tabs and jump to any one instantly.

No App Required

Just scan a QR code with your phone camera and you're connected. Works on any phone with a web browser - iPhone, Android, anything.

How It Works

Get started in under 30 seconds

1

Install the Chrome Extension

Add OrbitXE to your Chrome browser with one click. It's lightweight and runs in the background.

2

Scan the QR Code

Click the extension icon to reveal a QR code. Scan it with your phone's camera.

3

Start Controlling

Your phone is now a remote! Use the trackpad to move cursor, type with your keyboard, and more.
